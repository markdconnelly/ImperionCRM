# onboarding_step

A single step of a [`project`](project.md)'s onboarding playbook: the concrete checklist
the team works through to stand a new customer up. Each step has a **stable per-project
`code`** so automation can address it idempotently, an optional grouping under a
[`project_milestone`](project_milestone.md), and `deploy_key` / `verify_key` hooks that let
a backend provisioning stage *do* the step (deploy) and *confirm* it (verify) without a
human. Born silver â€” website system of record; the hard-coded playbook generator lives in
the app (`lib/onboarding-template.ts`). Governed by
[ADR-0037](../../../decision-records/ADR-0037-onboarding-playbook-template.md) and
[ADR-0020](../../../decision-records/ADR-0020-delivery-project-model.md).

## Source of record / authority

**Website system of record.** A step belongs to exactly one project (`project_id`) and is
**idempotent per `(project_id, code)`** (UNIQUE) â€” the `code` is the stable identity a
re-run of the playbook generator UPSERTs against, so regenerating a project's onboarding
never duplicates steps. `is_comm` marks a step as a customer-communication step (vs an
internal task). The `deploy_key` / `verify_key` name *which* automated deploy/verify hook
owns the step; `deploy_requested_at` records that a deploy was kicked off; `task_id` links
the step to the board [`task`](task.md) that represents it.
