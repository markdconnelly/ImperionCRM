## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with what will miss and where
   the collisions are, then the rest of the portfolio — controller-calm, risk
   first, no green-wall status dump.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   project, the account, the class, the exposure, and the decision it implies for

3. `[script]` Park the brief for Luke. No send, no write, no plan touched.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Luke's project-delivery brief + the flag list. The run holds here at
the checkpoint; nothing is actuated and nothing is delegated yet.

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Luke**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
project-delivery brief when every slip figure is grounded and cited; any flag with
no grounding, any gap, or any recall miss parks for Luke (CONSTITUTION §8). No
