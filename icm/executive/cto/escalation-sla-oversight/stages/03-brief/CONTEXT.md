# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Luke's escalation/SLA brief plus the flag list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the SLA-risk-bucketed, breach-ranked roll-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with what is about to breach and
   what is stuck, then the rest of the backlog roll-up by account — controller-calm,
   risk first.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   ticket, the account, the breach/age exposure, and the decision it implies for
   Luke (including which items are candidates to delegate to Felix, Sage, or Scout).
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Luke. No send, no write, no ticket touched.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Luke's escalation/SLA brief + the flag list. The run holds here at the
checkpoint; nothing is actuated and nothing is delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with about-to-breach and stuck items, not a gross open-ticket count
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the ticket, the account, the exposure, and the implied decision
- [ ] Delegate candidates are marked with their owning sub-agent but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — no ticket touched, no note written, no client contacted
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Dexter delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Luke**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
escalation/SLA brief when every breach figure is grounded and cited; any flag with
no grounding, any gap, or any recall miss parks for Luke (CONSTITUTION §8). No
actuation exists at this tier — Dexter advises, sub-agents act. The delegate to
Felix / Sage / Scout is the next stage, never folded into the brief.
