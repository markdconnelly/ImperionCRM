# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Luke's recurrence brief plus the flag list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the burn-ranked recurrence roll-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with what keeps coming back and
   what it costs (the highest-burn clusters), then the investigation and known-error
   state — controller-calm, risk first.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   cluster, its class (un-opened / stale / overdue-fix), the burn, and the decision
   it implies for Luke (including which clusters are candidates to delegate to Sage
   or Ozzie).
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Luke. No send, no write, no problem touched.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Luke's recurrence brief + the flag list. The run holds here at the
checkpoint; nothing is actuated and nothing is delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with the highest-burn recurring clusters, not a raw problem list
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the cluster, the class, the burn, and the implied decision
- [ ] Delegate candidates are marked with their owning sub-agent but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — no problem opened, advanced, or closed; no monitor touched
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Dexter delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Luke**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
recurrence brief when every cluster is grounded and cited; any flag with no
grounding, any gap, or any recall miss parks for Luke (CONSTITUTION §8). No
actuation exists at this tier — Dexter advises, Sage and Ozzie act. The delegate is
the next stage, never folded into the brief.
