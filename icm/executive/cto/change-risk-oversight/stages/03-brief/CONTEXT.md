# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Luke's change-risk brief plus the flag list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the risk-ranked change roll-up + defects |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with the dangerous change this
   week and the defects (freeze overlaps, rollback gaps, off-catalog standards),
   then the rest of the calendar — controller-calm, risk first, no drama.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   change, the defect class, the exposure, and the decision it implies for Luke
   (including which defects are candidates to delegate to Marshall for remediation).
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Luke. No send, no write, no change touched.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Luke's change-risk brief + the flag list. The run holds here at the
checkpoint; nothing is actuated and no remediation is delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with the dangerous change and the defects, not a raw calendar dump
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the change, the defect class, the exposure, and the implied decision
- [ ] Remediation candidates are marked but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — no change approved, scheduled, or modified
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Dexter delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Luke**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
change-risk brief when every calendar item is grounded and cited; any flag with no
grounding, any gap, or any recall miss parks for Luke (CONSTITUTION §8). No
actuation exists at this tier — Dexter advises, Marshall acts under his own gauntlet
where the freeze overlap is a hard block. The delegate to Marshall is the next
stage, never folded into the brief.
