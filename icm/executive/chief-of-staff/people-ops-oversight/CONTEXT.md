# Workflow: people-ops-oversight (Chief of Staff / Internal Ops, executive)

**Job:** on a schedule, synthesize the People/HR operational picture — Holly's
employee-lifecycle work in flight (onboarding, offboarding, records hygiene),
what's stuck, and what needs a human call — into a people-ops brief for Derek;
where a follow-up is grounded, delegate it to Holly. Rachel never touches an
employment/comp lever and never restates comp or personal data.

**Trigger:** scheduled (start of the working week). One run per cycle.

## Stages

| # | Stage | Job | Checkpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | gather | Pull Holly's run-ledger / handoff signals + parked checkpoints for the cycle | — |
| 02 | synthesize | Cluster by lifecycle theme, rank by staleness/exposure, isolate the flags | — |
| 03 | brief | Produce Derek's people-ops brief + the flag list; park | **Yes** |
| 04 | delegate-followups | OPTIONAL — propose a delegate to Holly / handoff to Nova; park | **Yes** |

## Autonomy

Executive ceiling is **L2 delegate-only** (CONSTITUTION §9): Rachel holds no
actuation tool. This workflow runs at that ceiling — it reads, synthesizes, parks
the brief for Derek, and MAY emit a **proposed** `delegate()` to Holly on a
grounded stalled lifecycle item or a `handoff()` to Nova when a flag spans
divisions. Employment/comp decisions and personal-data disclosure are always-gated
at the sub-agent tier and above (rachel.md §6): the brief never restates salary,
comp, or personal data — it references the item, never the values. Rachel never
actuates; every effect re-gates inside Holly's own gauntlet.

## Runtime skills

None (Tier 3 empty). The job is read + synthesize + brief + route; voice and
guardrails come from the composed Rachel persona. Rules of the format:
`../../../CONVENTIONS.md`. The structured manifest is `agent.yaml`; the composed
workflow prose is `prose.md`.
