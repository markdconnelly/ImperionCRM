# Stage 04 — delegate-followups

**Job:** OPTIONAL — propose a `delegate()` to Holly on a grounded flag and/or a
`handoff()` to Nova when it spans divisions, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brief + flags | stage 03 `brief.md` | the flag list only | what may warrant a follow-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` For each flag, decide the route: a stalled people-domain lifecycle
   item → Holly; a flag spanning divisions → Nova; an employment/comp decision →
   neither (it parks for Derek — decisions that bind the company route to a human,
   CONSTITUTION §5.4). A flag that is not grounded and cited also parks.
2. `[sonnet]` Draft the proposed `delegate()` to Holly: the item reference, the
   age, the cited basis, and the ask — never the HR action itself. Draft any
   `handoff()` to Nova the same way.
3. `[script]` Park the proposed delegate/handoff. No send, no write, no HR record
   touched. The effect re-gates inside Holly's own gauntlet.

## Outputs

`followups.md` — the proposed `delegate()` / `handoff()` items by reference, each
naming the target, the item, the age, and the cited basis. The run parks here;
nothing is actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Every proposed delegate/handoff names a target and cites the grounding basis
- [ ] An ungrounded or uncited flag parks for Derek — never delegated
- [ ] Every employment/comp decision parks for Derek — never delegated
- [ ] No comp, salary, or personal-data VALUE appears anywhere
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Rachel delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The proposed follow-ups park for **Derek**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY
delegating a flagged stalled lifecycle item to Holly when that flag is grounded
and cited; any employment/comp decision, ungrounded flag, gap, or recall miss
parks for Derek (CONSTITUTION §8). Rachel never actuates — the delegate routes the
work and the effect re-gates inside Holly's gauntlet (CONSTITUTION §9).
