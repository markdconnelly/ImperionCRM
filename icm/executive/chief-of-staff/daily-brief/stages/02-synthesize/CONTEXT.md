# Stage 02 — synthesize

**Job:** turn the gather record into a prioritized picture with the
decisions-needed items isolated.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gather record | stage 01 `gather.md` | all | the raw material |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Cluster the signals into themes; collapse duplicates across
   divisions.
2. `[sonnet]` Rank each cluster by what's blocked or decision-ready; demote pure
   status.
3. `[sonnet]` Isolate the items that need a human's decision today, each with the
   one question Derek must answer.

## Outputs

`synthesis.md` — ranked clusters (blocked / decision-ready first) and a separate
decisions-needed list, each item naming the division and the one decision required.

## Audit

- [ ] Clusters are ranked, blocked/decision-ready leading
- [ ] Every decisions-needed item names a division and one decision
- [ ] No cluster restates the gather list verbatim (it must be synthesized)
