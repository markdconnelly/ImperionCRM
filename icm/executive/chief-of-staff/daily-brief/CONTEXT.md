# Workflow: daily-brief (Chief of Staff / Internal Ops, executive tracer)

**Job:** every working day, roll up cross-division status, priorities, and
blockers into one short brief for Derek, plus the decisions-needed list — drafted
here, never actuated.

**Trigger:** scheduled (start of the working day). One run per day.

## Stages

| # | Stage | Job | Checkpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | gather | Pull cross-division status + the accounts/contacts in play | — |
| 02 | synthesize | Cluster, prioritize, isolate decisions-needed | — |
| 03 | brief | Produce Derek's brief + the decisions-needed list; park | **Yes** |

## Autonomy

Executive ceiling is **L2 delegate-only** (CONSTITUTION §9): Rachel holds no
actuation tool. This tracer runs narrower still — **L1 propose**: it reads,
synthesizes, and parks the brief for Derek. It never delegates, sends, or writes;
every effect remains a sub-agent's under its own gauntlet. The brief is a
checkpoint, not an action.

## Runtime skills

None (Tier 3 empty). The job is read + synthesize + park; the voice and
guardrails come from the composed Rachel persona. Rules of the format:
`../../../CONVENTIONS.md`. The structured manifest is `agent.yaml`; the composed
tracer prose is `prose.md`.
