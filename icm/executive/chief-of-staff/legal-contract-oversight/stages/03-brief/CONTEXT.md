# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Derek's legal/contract brief plus the flag list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the lapse-ranked legal/contract picture |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with what lapses or binds
   soonest (the ninety-second read — answer first, the two things that matter,
   the one decision needed), then the rest of the contract picture — never an
   inventory dump.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   agreement, the counterparty, the deadline or exposure, and the follow-up it
   implies for Derek.
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Derek. No send, no write, nothing executed or
   bound.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Derek's legal/contract brief + the flag list. The run parks here at
the checkpoint; nothing is actuated. Flags feed the optional stage 04.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with the soonest lapse / largest obligation, never an inventory dump
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the agreement, counterparty, deadline/exposure, and implied follow-up
- [ ] Nothing was executed, signed, renewed, or terminated
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Rachel delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Derek**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the
scheduled brief when every item is grounded and cited; any gap or recall miss
parks for Derek (CONSTITUTION §8). No actuation exists at this tier — Rachel
advises and routes, sub-agents act; binding the company is always a human's call
(CONSTITUTION §5.4).
