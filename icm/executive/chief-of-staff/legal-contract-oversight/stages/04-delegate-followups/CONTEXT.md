# Stage 04 — delegate-followups

**Job:** OPTIONAL — propose a `delegate()` to Laurel on a grounded flag and/or a
`handoff()` to Nova when it spans divisions, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brief + flags | stage 03 `brief.md` | the flag list only | what may warrant a follow-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` For each flag, decide the route: an expiring/at-risk or un-reviewed
   agreement → Laurel (a review or redline ask); a flag spanning divisions (a
   renewal with commercial terms, a vendor dispute touching Finance) → Nova.
   Anything that would execute, sign, or bind the company routes to neither — it
   parks for Derek (CONSTITUTION §5.4). An ungrounded or uncited flag also parks.
2. `[sonnet]` Draft the proposed `delegate()` to Laurel: the agreement, the
   counterparty, the deadline or exposure, the cited basis, and the review ask —
   never the execution. Draft any `handoff()` to Nova the same way.
3. `[script]` Park the proposed delegate/handoff. No send, no write, nothing
   executed or bound. The review re-gates inside Laurel's own gauntlet.

## Outputs

`followups.md` — the proposed `delegate()` / `handoff()` items by reference, each
naming the target, the agreement, the counterparty, the deadline/exposure, and the
cited basis. The run parks here; nothing is actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Every proposed delegate/handoff names a target and cites the grounding basis
- [ ] An ungrounded or uncited flag parks for Derek — never delegated
- [ ] Anything that executes, signs, or binds the company parks for a human — never delegated
- [ ] No outreach, commitment, or content was drafted toward any counterparty
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Rachel delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The proposed follow-ups park for **Derek**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY
delegating a flagged expiring/at-risk agreement to Laurel for review when that
flag is grounded and cited; anything binding, any ungrounded flag, gap, or recall
miss parks for Derek (CONSTITUTION §8). Rachel never actuates — the delegate
routes the review and it re-gates inside Laurel's gauntlet (CONSTITUTION §9);
binding the company is always a human's call (CONSTITUTION §5.4).
