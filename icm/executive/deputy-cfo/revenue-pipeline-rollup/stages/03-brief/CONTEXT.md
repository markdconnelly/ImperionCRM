## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the rollup: a short read leading with slippage and at-risk
   commits (stalled opportunities, slipping close dates, soft renewals), then the
   rest of the forecast picture — never a vanity bookings number.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the rollup's action surface — each item the
   account/opportunity, the exposure, and the follow-up it implies for Nick.
3. `[script]` Park the rollup for Nick. No send, no write. Finance stays

checkpoint; nothing is actuated. Flags feed the optional stage 04.
