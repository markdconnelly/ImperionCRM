# Stage 04 — delegate-followups

**Job:** OPTIONAL — propose a `delegate()` to Chase on a grounded flag and/or a
`handoff()` to Nova when it spans divisions, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rollup + flags | stage 03 `brief.md` | the flag list only | what may warrant a follow-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` For each flag, decide the route: a single stalled/slipping
   opportunity → Chase (Sales); a flag spanning divisions → Nova. A flag that is
   not grounded and cited routes to neither — it parks for Nick.
2. `[sonnet]` Draft the proposed `delegate()` to Chase: name the account/opportunity,
   the exposure, the cited basis, and the ask — never the outreach itself. Draft
   any `handoff()` to Nova the same way.
3. `[script]` Park the proposed delegate/handoff. No send, no write, no outreach,
   no commitment. The world-changing effect re-gates inside Chase's own gauntlet.

## Outputs

`followups.md` — the proposed `delegate()` / `handoff()` items by reference, each
naming the target sub-agent, the account/opportunity, the exposure, and the cited
basis. The run parks here; nothing is actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Every proposed delegate/handoff names a target and cites the grounding basis
- [ ] An ungrounded or uncited flag parks for Nick — never delegated
- [ ] No outreach, commitment, or content was drafted toward any client
- [ ] Read-only — no financial record written, no money moved
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Sterling delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The proposed follow-ups park for **Nick**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY delegating
a flagged stalled opportunity to Chase when that flag is grounded and cited; any
ungrounded flag, gap, or recall miss parks for Nick (CONSTITUTION §8). Sterling
never sends, writes, or actuates — the delegate routes the work and the
world-changing effect re-gates inside Chase's gauntlet (CONSTITUTION §9); finance
never leaves read-only.
