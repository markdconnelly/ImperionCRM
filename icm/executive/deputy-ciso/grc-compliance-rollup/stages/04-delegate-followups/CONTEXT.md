# Stage 04 — delegate-followups

**Job:** OPTIONAL — propose a `delegate()` to Grace on a grounded gap and/or a
`handoff()` to Nova when it spans divisions, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brief + gaps | stage 03 `brief.md` | the gap list only | what may warrant a follow-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` For each gap, decide the route: an evidence collection or
   control-gap investigation → Grace (GRC); a detection-coverage gap → Cyrus; an
   access-hygiene gap → Osiris; a gap spanning divisions → Nova. A gap that is
   not grounded and cited routes to nowhere — it parks for Mark.
2. `[sonnet]` Draft the proposed `delegate()`: name the framework/tenant by id,
   the gap, the cited basis, and the ask — never the remediation itself. Draft
   any `handoff()` to Nova the same way.
3. `[script]` Park the proposed delegate/handoff. No control change, no
   remediation, no send, no write. The world-changing effect re-gates inside
   the owning report's gauntlet (always-gated, ADR-0128).

## Outputs

`followups.md` — the proposed `delegate()` / `handoff()` items by reference,
each naming the target report, the framework/tenant, the gap, and the cited
basis. The run parks here; nothing is actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Every proposed delegate/handoff names a target report and cites the grounding basis
- [ ] An ungrounded or uncited gap parks for Mark — never delegated
- [ ] No control change, remediation, or attestation proposed as Roman's own
- [ ] No client PII, no secret values present (audit by reference)
- [ ] No send/write/actuation occurred — Roman delegated or parked

## Checkpoint

The proposed follow-ups park for **Mark**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY
delegating a grounded evidence/control-gap follow-up to Grace when it is
grounded and cited; any ungrounded gap, ambiguity, or recall miss parks for
Mark (CONSTITUTION §8). Roman never changes a control or actuates — the
delegate routes the work and the effect re-gates inside the owning report's
gauntlet (CONSTITUTION §9); a report's always-gate bar never lowers because
Roman's dial is high (ADR-0128).
