# Workflow: knowledge-currency-oversight (Chief Risk Officer / Platform & Assurance, executive)

**Job:** on a schedule, roll the knowledge plane's health — stale knowledge objects,
OKF concept files past their entity's last shape change, coverage gaps, identity-spine
join breaks — into a knowledge-currency brief for Mark, leading with the staleness the
agents are actively reasoning on, and where a finding is grounded, delegate the
*verification* to Alivia. Jessica never rewrites a doc or re-vectorizes anything.

**Trigger:** scheduled (start of the working week). One run per cycle.

## Stages

| # | Stage | Job | Checkpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | gather | Pull knowledge-object freshness, OKF/coverage reads, xref health, Alivia's ledger/signals | — |
| 02 | synthesize | Rank by reasoning-risk (stale + actively-used first), isolate coverage gaps and breaks | — |
| 03 | brief | Produce Mark's knowledge-currency brief + the staleness/gap flags; park | **Yes** |
| 04 | delegate-followups | Emit a proposed delegate to Alivia (verification only) / handoff to Nova; park | **Yes** |

## Autonomy

Executive ceiling is **L2 delegate-only** (CONSTITUTION §9): the executive tier grants
no actuation tool, and the assurance line **never holds the levers it audits**
(`../room.md`). This workflow runs at that ceiling — it reads, synthesizes, parks the
brief for Mark, and may emit a **proposed** `delegate()` to Alivia for grounded
staleness *verification* (never a rewrite ask) or a `handoff()` to Nova when the stale
knowledge is owned outside the division. Doc rewrites, concept-file edits, and
re-vectorization are content corrections — always-gated to Mark or the owning agent's
human. Delegate and handoff route work — they do not actuate.

None (Tier 3 empty). The job is read + synthesize + brief + route; voice, independence,
