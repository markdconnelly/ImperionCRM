# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Mark's knowledge-currency brief plus the staleness / gap / break
flag list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the reasoning-risk-ranked roll-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with the stale knowledge agents
   are actively reasoning on, then the lagging OKF concepts, coverage gaps, and spine
   breaks — findings stated plainly, no softening (jessica.md §4).
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   knowledge item / entity, the finding (verified/suspected) by reference, and the
   decision it implies for Mark (owner rewrite, concept-file update, re-vectorize, or
   xref repair — all levers held elsewhere, none Jessica's).
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Mark. Nothing rewritten, nothing re-vectorized.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Mark's knowledge-currency brief + the flag list. The run holds here at
the checkpoint; nothing is actuated and no verification is delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with stale-and-actively-used knowledge, not a raw staleness count
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the item/entity, the finding by reference, and the implied Mark decision
- [ ] Verified vs suspected labeling carried through from synthesis
- [ ] No finding softened, dropped, or downgraded to spare a team — independence holds (jessica.md §2/§6)
- [ ] Verification candidates are marked but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — nothing rewritten, nothing re-vectorized

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Mark**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
knowledge-currency brief when every finding is grounded and cited by reference; any
uncited finding, any gap, or any recall miss parks for Mark (CONSTITUTION §8). No
actuation exists at this tier — doc rewrites, concept-file edits, and
re-vectorization are content corrections gated to Mark or the owning agent's human.
The delegate to Alivia is the next stage, never folded into the brief.
