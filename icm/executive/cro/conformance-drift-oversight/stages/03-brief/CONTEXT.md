list, then park.

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with the highest-risk drift and
   the quarantine candidates, then the rest of the conformance roll-up by
   agent/workflow — findings stated plainly, no softening (jessica.md §4).
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   agent/workflow, the finding (verified/suspected) by reference, and the decision
   it implies for Mark (correction, dial change, ratification, or quarantine — all

3. `[script]` Park the brief for Mark. No fix, no config change, no dial touched.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Mark's conformance-drift brief + the flag list. The run holds here at
the checkpoint; nothing is actuated and no verification is delegated yet.

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Mark**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
conformance-drift brief when every finding is grounded and cited by reference; any
