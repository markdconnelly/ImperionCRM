# Stage 04 — delegate-followups

**Job:** optionally emit a proposed `delegate()` to the owning watcher for grounded
observation and/or a `handoff()` to Nova for out-of-division escalations, then park —
Jessica never contains, corrects, or quarantines.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Queue brief | stage 03 `brief.md` | the ranked, owner-identified items | what may warrant routing |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` For each grounded item owned by a division watcher, draft a
   **proposed** `delegate()` to **Vera** (platform/conformance), **Tess** (service
   quality), or **Alivia** (knowledge): the escalation id, the subject id, and the
   evidence references — an *observation/verification* ask only; never containment,
   correction, or quarantine (the watchers observe too; they hold no levers either).
2. `[sonnet]` For each item owned outside Platform & Assurance (an active security
   incident, a delivery failure, a money matter), draft a `handoff()` to **Nova**,
   naming the owning division and the reason — never delegated sideways.
3. `[script]` Attach each delegate/handoff to the queue as a proposal and park.
   Critical items and every quarantine/containment/correction decision remain with
   Mark; nothing is enacted here.

## Outputs

`followups.md` — the proposed `delegate()` calls to the owning watchers and/or
`handoff()` calls to Nova, each citing the escalation and subject ids and the
evidence references. The run ends here at the checkpoint; any world-changing action
happens on Mark's decision or inside another division's own gauntlet, never here.

## Audit

- [ ] Every delegate names the escalation id, the subject id, and its evidence references
- [ ] Every delegate is an observation/verification ask — no containment, correction, or quarantine ask routed to anyone
- [ ] Out-of-division items are handed off to Nova with the owning division named — never delegated sideways
- [ ] Critical items are left with Mark — not delegated at all
- [ ] No client PII reproduced — everything by reference
- [ ] Read-only — nothing contained, corrected, or quarantined

## Checkpoint

The follow-ups park for **Mark**, and any delegate is a **proposal** to a watcher.
`auto` may self-approve ONLY emitting the `delegate()` to the owning watcher for
grounded observation, and the `handoff()` to Nova for out-of-division items; critical
items always stay with Mark, and quarantine, containment, correction, and governance
changes are always-gated to him (CONSTITUTION §9, jessica.md §6). The assurance line
never holds the levers it audits; any ungrounded or out-of-scope item parks for Mark.
