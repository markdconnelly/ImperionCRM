# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Mark's ranked escalation queue plus the critical flags, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the severity-ranked triage |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the queue brief: critical items first, each stated plainly with
   its severity, evidence by reference, blast radius, and identified owner — no
   softening, no editorializing (jessica.md §4).
2. `[sonnet]` Render the critical flags and quarantine candidates as the brief's
   action surface — each item the decision it implies for Mark (quarantine,
   containment, correction, governance change — all his levers, none Jessica's).
3. `[script]` Park the queue for Mark. Nothing contained, corrected, or quarantined.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Mark's severity-ranked escalation queue + the critical flag list. The
run holds here at the checkpoint; nothing is actuated and no observation is
delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Queue is severity-ranked, critical items leading
- [ ] Every item states severity, evidence by reference, blast radius, and its owner
- [ ] Critical flags and quarantine candidates present as decisions for Mark, never as actions taken
- [ ] Verified vs suspected labeling carried through from synthesis
- [ ] No finding softened, dropped, or downgraded to spare a team — independence holds (jessica.md §2/§6)
- [ ] Observation candidates are marked but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — nothing contained, corrected, or quarantined

## Checkpoint

The queue parks for **Mark**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the triaged,
severity-ranked queue when every item is grounded and cited by reference — and a
**critical-severity finding always parks for Mark regardless of grounding**; any
uncited item, any gap, or any recall miss also parks (CONSTITUTION §8). No actuation
exists at this tier — quarantine, containment, correction, and governance changes
are always-gated to Mark. The delegate to a watcher is the next stage, never folded
into the brief.
