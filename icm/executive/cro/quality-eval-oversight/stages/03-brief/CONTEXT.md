# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Mark's quality/eval brief plus the regression / coverage-gap flag
list, then park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the baseline-compared, regression-ranked roll-up |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the brief: a short read leading with the regressions and the
   coverage gaps, then the rest of the quality roll-up by module — findings stated
   plainly, no softening (jessica.md §4).
2. `[sonnet]` Render the flag list as the brief's action surface — each item the
   module, the measured figure or gap by reference, and the decision it implies for
   Mark (golden authoring, baseline move, threshold change, or quarantine — all his
   levers, none Jessica's).
3. `[script]` Park the brief for Mark. No golden edited, no baseline moved, no run
   re-scored.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Mark's quality/eval brief + the flag list. The run holds here at the
checkpoint; nothing is actuated and no observation is delegated yet.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with the regressions and the coverage gaps, not a gross pass-rate average
- [ ] Flag list present; each item states the module, the figure/gap by reference, and the implied Mark decision
- [ ] Signal-vs-inference labeling carried through from synthesis
- [ ] No finding softened, dropped, or downgraded to spare a team — independence holds (jessica.md §2/§6)
- [ ] Observation candidates are marked but not yet delegated (that is stage 04)
- [ ] Read-only — no golden edited, no baseline moved, no run re-scored

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Mark**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
quality/eval brief when every score and gap is grounded and cited by reference; any
uncited figure, any gap in the data, or any recall miss parks for Mark
(CONSTITUTION §8). No actuation exists at this tier — golden edits, baseline moves,
and threshold changes are governance changes, always-gated to Mark. The delegate to
Tess is the next stage, never folded into the brief.
