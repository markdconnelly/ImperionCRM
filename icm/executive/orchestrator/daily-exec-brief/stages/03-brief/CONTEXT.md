# Stage 03 — brief

**Job:** produce Derek's one-page exec brief plus the decisions-needed list, then
park.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 `synthesis.md` | all | the clustered, company-ranked picture + cross-division flags + decisions-needed |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Write the one-page exec brief in Nova's voice, leading with the
   decisions-needed and the cross-division flags — the apex picture, not a stack of
   the five division briefs reprinted. Carry each item's citation through so Derek can
   drill to the source brief.
2. `[sonnet]` Render the decisions-needed list as the brief's action surface — each
   item the exposure, the decision it implies, and the **owning human** it is routed
   to (not defaulted to Derek).
3. `[script]` Carry any gap from stage 01 (a missing or stale division brief) into the
   brief as a stated gap, never silently dropped. Any section that could not be
   grounded is stated as "no data," never filled in.
4. `[script]` Park the brief for Derek. No send, no delegate, no write — every real
   effect already happened (or parked) one tier down under each division agent's
   gauntlet.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — Derek's one-page exec brief + the decisions-needed list (each routed to
its owning human). The run ends here at the checkpoint; nothing is actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Brief leads with decisions-needed and cross-division flags, not a reprint of the five division briefs
- [ ] Citations carried through to each source division brief; any ungrounded section stated as "no data," not invented
- [ ] Decisions-needed list present; each item states the exposure, the implied decision, and the owning human (none defaulted to Derek)
- [ ] Any stage-01 gap (missing/stale division brief) carried into the brief, not silently dropped
- [ ] Pool-never-bleed held — no other client's or owner's data surfaced (two-axis RLS)
- [ ] No send, delegate, or write occurred — the run parked

## Checkpoint

The brief parks for **Derek**. `auto` may self-approve ONLY publishing the scheduled
daily exec brief when every section is grounded and cited; any flag, gap, or recall
miss parks for Derek (CONSTITUTION §8). No actuation exists at this tier — Nova
synthesizes, sub-agents act one tier down under their own gauntlets, and a roll-up
never re-opens or bypasses a sub-agent's gauntlet.
