## 1. Purpose
This program governs the commercial and back-office operations that run the company —
demand/marketing, sales and pricing, procurement, client success, billing and finance, legal
contracting, and people operations. It binds human staff and business agents to honest,
consent-respecting, commitment-disciplined, financially-controlled conduct.

## 2. Scope
All commercial/back-office actors (human and agent) and the Operating Procedures in Streams 01
(Demand → Lead), 02 (Lead → Cash), 08 (Engage → Retain), 09 (Record → Report), and the G&A
procedures in Stream 10 (Run the Company). Finance is read-only/advisory where QuickBooks Online
is the system of record.

## 4. Application to autonomous agents (category-wide)
- **Money and external commitment are `always_gate`.** Ad spend (Belle), purchases/renewals/
  cancellations (Vance), pricing/discount/quote commitments (Chase), and any movement of money
  (Audrey) require a human decision at every dial level — the agent prepares the full setup and
  presents a one-click easy-button; the human commits.
- **No fabrication, no false urgency, consent is absolute.** Agents never invent pricing,
  timelines, capabilities, claims, testimonials, or stats; respect opt-outs/frequency caps;
  never 1:1-DM an existing customer (Belle refusal-class).
- **No commitments by Client Success.** Celeste advises (no-commits-ever ceiling); all binding
  commitments (roadmap/SLA/spend) route to a human. MSSP work is advisory-only.
- **Finance is advisory/read-only.** Audrey detects/drafts/ages and advises (tops at L2); QBO
  is SoR; the collections **send** and any posting/invoice change are human `always_gate`;
  salary is non-disclosure / refusal-class.
- **HR & Legal** agents (Holly, Laurel) draft and route; hiring/termination, contract execution,
  and sanctions are human decisions.

## 5. Enforcement & audit
