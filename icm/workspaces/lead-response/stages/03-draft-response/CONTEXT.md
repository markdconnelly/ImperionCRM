# Stage 03 — draft-response

**Job:** produce one channel-correct, on-brand response draft with a rationale.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Triage + dossier | stages 01/02 outputs | all | substance |
| Voice | `../../skills/voice-and-tone.md` | all | how we sound |
| Offers | `../../skills/offer-catalog.md` | the suggested angle's entry | what we propose |
| Channel rules | `../../skills/channel-rules.md` | the lead's channel section | hard constraints |

## Process

1. `[haiku]` Select channel per `channel-rules.md` (email default; in-channel
   DM for DM-sourced leads inside the messaging window; Apollo leads follow
   the nurture sequence step, not ad-hoc drafting).
2. `[sonnet]` Draft the response: answer what was asked, one clear next step
   (booking link / reply / call), voice-and-tone compliant, length per channel
   rules. Never invent pricing, availability, or capabilities — pricing intent
   gets the `pricing-contract` handling note, not numbers.
3. `[sonnet]` Write a 2–3 sentence rationale: why this angle, why this CTA,
   what was deliberately left out.

## Outputs

`draft.md` — channel, recipient address/handle, subject (email only), body,
rationale, the offer id used.

## Audit

- [ ] Channel choice legal per `channel-rules.md` (window, consent basis)
- [ ] No pricing/contract commitments in the body
- [ ] Exactly one CTA; voice checklist passes
- [ ] Body length within the channel's limit
