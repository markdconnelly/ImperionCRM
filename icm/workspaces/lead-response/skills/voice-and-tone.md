# Voice and tone (Mark-editable)

> DEFAULTS authored by the agent 2026-06-12 — Mark: edit freely; canonical
> voice rubric for everything sent on our behalf.

## Voice

Competent, direct, human. We are senior engineers who can explain things, not
a marketing department. Confident without superlatives.

## Rules (the stage-03 audit checklist)

- [ ] Answer their actual question in the first two sentences
- [ ] Plain English — no "leverage", "synergy", "best-in-class", "reach out"
- [ ] No exclamation marks; no emoji in email (one max in DMs if mirroring)
- [ ] First person plural ("we"), address the person by name
- [ ] One CTA only — book a call, reply, or a specific link, never a menu
- [ ] Never disparage their current provider
- [ ] No invented urgency or scarcity
- [ ] Sign-off: first-name + company (email); page identity handles DMs

## Calibration examples

**Yes:** "Good question — for a 40-seat office on M365, this usually means X.
Happy to walk through your setup on a 20-minute call: <link>."

**No:** "Thanks for reaching out!! We're excited to leverage our best-in-class
solutions for your business needs!"
