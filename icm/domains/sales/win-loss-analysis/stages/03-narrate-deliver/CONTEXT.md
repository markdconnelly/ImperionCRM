# Stage 03 — narrate-deliver

**Job:** narrate the win-loss brief and apply the B3 launchpad rule — pre-staging the owning
worker procedures PARKED — then deliver the brief to its consumers and log. Terminal stage.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synthesis | stage 02 output | the cohort | the win/loss patterns to narrate |
| Gathered cohort | stage 01 output | the cohort | the stale-source flags to carry through |
| Source campaigns | `` `okf:campaign` `` | attributed campaigns | naming the source/demand owner per loss-on-demand pattern |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Narrate the brief (P2 attribution): why deals close or slip by segment/competitor/
   source, each claim citing its pattern + as-of (A5). Carry the stage-01 stale-source flags
   through so a stale source reads as stale, not as a real finding.
2. `[script]` Apply the **B3 launchpad rule** — a finding pre-stages the owning worker procedure
   **PARKED** for one-click launch, never actuated: a recurring loss-on-price pattern pre-stages
   **02-C1** (rate-card review) parked; a loss-on-demand pattern pre-stages a **Belle Stream-01**
   procedure parked. The launchpad is staged; this stage launches nothing.
3. `[script]` **Deliver** (A6 routing) → Sterling (02-C4 forecast) + Belle (demand) + the 09-17
   roll-up; **log**. **No actuation here:** this stage opens no action, writes no silver, sends
   nothing.

## Outputs

`brief.md` — the narrated win-loss brief (patterns by segment/competitor/source, cited), the
pre-staged PARKED launchpad entries (02-C1 / Belle Stream-01) for a human to launch, the carried
stale-source flags, and the delivery routing — and nothing actuated.

## Audit

- [ ] Stale-source flags carried through and labeled stale, not finding
- [ ] Every launchpad entry pre-staged PARKED + names its owning procedure (02-C1 / Belle Stream-01) — nothing launched
- [ ] Delivered to Sterling + Belle + the 09-17 roll-up and logged; no send, no silver write, no action opened (read-and-flag only)
