## Fit score rubric (1–5)

| Score | Profile |
|---|---|
| 5 | SMB/mid-market, 10–250 seats, M365-centric, regulated or security-sensitive (finance, health, legal, manufacturing), seeking managed services or co-managed IT |
| 4 | As above but smaller (5–10 seats) or not yet M365-centric |
| 3 | Project-only intent (migration, audit, one-off) — viable, not the core motion |
| 2 | Residential/consumer, or outside serviceable geography |
| 1 | Reseller spam, job seekers, vendors selling to us |

## Segment notes (angle selection, stage 02)

- **Security-sensitive verticals:** lead with posture/assessment offers, not
  generic "we do IT".
- **Co-managed prospects (existing IT staff):** lead with augmentation, never
  replacement.
- **Project-only:** answer the project ask directly; nurture toward managed
  later, not in the first reply.
