# Stage 03 — assemble-pack

**Job:** assemble the QBR/TBR pack and park every commitment for the human facilitator.

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Period recap | `recap.md` (stage 01 output) | full | the recap section |
| Health & forward | `forward.md` (stage 02 output) | full | the health + agenda sections |
| Pack rubric | `./skills/qbr-agenda.md` | all | pack structure + recommendation discipline |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Assemble the four pack sections per `qbr-agenda.md`: period recap, health &
   posture, forward agenda, and talking points + recommendations.
2. `[sonnet]` Write every binding commitment in the pack — roadmap, SLA, pricing, spend,
   security-remediation — as a **parked recommendation for the human facilitator**, never
   as agreed. Flag any **non-interest upsell** (guardrail 4). Real expansion value mints an
   opportunity for Chase (the Chase ↔ Celeste seam). Security stays advisory.
3. `[script]` Mark the disposition: the QBR pack + the parked recommendation list, each
   tagged with its owner (Celeste draft → human facilitator, or → Chase for expansion).
   Nothing is committed or sent here.

## Outputs

`pack.md` — the QBR/TBR pack (the four sections) + the parked recommendation list.
Terminal stage; the run ends parked for the human facilitator.

## Audit

- [ ] All four pack sections present (recap · health/posture · forward agenda · talking points)
- [ ] Every binding commitment is parked for the facilitator, not decided
- [ ] Any non-interest upsell is flagged, not buried
- [ ] No client-facing send emitted; the review is human-facilitated

## Checkpoint

The Teams loop: a human co-shapes and approves the pack before the review. **`auto` (L2)
may self-approve the internal pack assembly + recap/health compute ONLY** — every
recommendation, binding commitment, and client-facing touch parks for a human, and a
**human facilitates the review** (NO-COMMITS-EVER, dial-proof; MSSP work is advisory-only
— remediation is human/Datto).
