# Stage 02 — assess-readiness

**Job:** assess health/risk + the renewal-readiness dimensions (signal vs inference).

## Inputs

| Source | Location | Scope | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Renewal context | `renewal-context.md` (stage 01) | full | the renewal + account picture |
| Readiness rubric | `./skills/renewal-readiness-rubric.md` | all | the readiness dimensions + posture rule |
| Health rubric | `../client-360/skills/health-signals.md` | all | the shared signal-vs-inference rubric |

## Process

1. `[sonnet]` Assess each readiness dimension (health/engagement, service track record,
   strategic alignment, commercial fit via Audrey's margin handoff, sentiment). For every
   verdict, **label measured signal vs your inference** (celeste.md guardrail 3).
2. `[sonnet]` Fold in Audrey's margin handoff as **context, not a price** — you read no
   financials directly. Form the readiness read: ready / needs-attention / at-risk.

## Outputs

`readiness.md` — the per-dimension readiness verdicts (signal vs inference labeled) and the
overall readiness read.
