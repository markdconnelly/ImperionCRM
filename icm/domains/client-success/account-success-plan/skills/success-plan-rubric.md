## The plan structure (the five sections, in order)

1. **Relationship goals** — where this account should be in 12 months, framed as the
   *client's* outcomes (resilience, growth enablement, risk reduction), not Imperion's
   revenue. 2–4 goals; more is noise.
2. **Health & trajectory** — the current health read + the direction (improving /
   steady / drifting / at-risk), each with the measured signals behind it.
3. **Strategic initiatives** — the prioritized moves that serve the goals (a refresh, a
   posture uplift, an expansion, a QBR cadence). Each names the goal it serves.
4. **Open risks** — churn-risk, service friction, posture gaps, renewal risk — each with
   its source signal and a parked mitigation recommendation.
5. **Next actions** — owner · due · the parked recommendation. Nothing here is committed;
   every action is a proposal a human owns.

## The discipline: signal vs inference

- **Measured signal** = a fact from a source row: usage down (interaction/telemetry),
  rising tickets (ticket), margin eroded (Audrey handoff), no QBR in 9 months
  (strategic_business_review), renewal approaching (opportunity `kind=renewal`).
- **Inference** = Celeste's reading ("this account is consolidating spend with us").
- **Always label which is which.** A trajectory call states the signals that produced it.
  A goal or risk asserted without evidence is not a plan — it is a guess (celeste.md
  guardrail 3).

## Goal & initiative quality

- **In the client's interest, not Imperion's revenue.** Flag a **non-interest upsell**
  explicitly — never recommend spend purely to grow revenue (guardrail 4).
- **Prioritize by client impact × feasibility**, not by deal size. Name the rejected
  alternative when you down-rank an initiative.
- **Propose, never commit.** Roadmap / SLA / pricing / spend / security-remediation are
  recommendations to a human, at every level (guardrail 1, dial-proof).
- **Expansion → Chase.** Real expansion value mints an opportunity handed to Chase; you
  plan the relationship, Chase closes the transaction.
- **Security = advisory only.** Posture / risk / recommendations in the plan; remediation
  is human / Datto (guardrail 2, MSSP boundary).
